# Phenotype (Prosopa's calibration baseline)

Prosopa opens every reading with phenotype. His doctrine: a feature's meaning is **relative to the phenotypic baseline**, not absolute. A "long nose" on a Gracile Mediterranid is unremarkable; the same nose on an Alpinid is a significant divergence. Without phenotype, you can't tell which features are individual signals vs collective traits of the population.

This reference covers the framework, the diagnostic feature stack he uses to identify phenotype, and his taxonomy of major -id labels.

## Phenotype vs race

Prosopa explicitly distinguishes the two: "Phenotype, which was in the past often sloppily and inaccurately called your race, is a visible marker of the past 10,000 years or more of your ancestors' evolution." Race is the fuzzy older umbrella; phenotype is the narrower, evidence-driven replacement. Read phenotype as a proxy for evolutionary lineage, not as a social category.

## Evolutionary mechanisms (the "Science" framing)

Five mechanisms Prosopa names as driving phenotypic divergence:

1. **Climate adaptation** — Allen's rule (hot climates → longer thinner limbs; cold → shorter thicker) + Bergmann's rule (cold → larger specimens). Used to explain Maasai vs Inuit body plans, East African running vs Northeast Asian powerlifting, skin pigmentation (melanin / vitamin D), East-Asian facial-hair loss (ice on hair in cold), Sub-Saharan body-hair loss (anti-parasite).
2. **Founder effect** — small migrant subgroups over-represent ancestral traits. Explains why epicanthal folds, flat faces, reduced facial hair persist in Chinese / Southeast Asians / Native Americans who no longer live in extreme cold.
3. **Isolation** — small isolated populations diverge.
4. **Sexual selection** — Irish elk antlers as illustration. Human female permanently distended breasts likely a sexual-selection signal unique among primates. "We as a species were literally so horny that it may have affected our evolution."
5. **Interbreeding with archaic hominids** — Europeans ~10–20% Neanderthal; Melanesians / Australian Aborigines carry Denisovan DNA; Sub-Saharan Africans may carry up to ~30% from a "ghost population."

## Diagnostic feature stack (the order to assess phenotype)

1. **Skin color, hair color, hair texture** — visible but lower-weight (most environmentally plastic).
2. **Skull shape** — dolichocephalic (long, narrow) vs brachycephalic (short, broad). The single most diagnostic trait. Note also large vs small skull volume — Nordid is long-narrow-and-large; Mediterranid is long-narrow-and-small.
3. **Nose** — length, breadth, concave vs convex profile.
4. **Body** — height, body type, limb-to-torso proportions.

Most diagnostic features are skeletal — anthropologists ascertain phenotype from bones alone.

## How to use phenotype in a reading

- **Identify the phenotype first** — before reading any individual feature.
- **State the call openly** — "this reads as a North Atlantid (Nordid × Atlanto-Mediterranid contact type) — Henry Cavill complexion."
- **Use it to calibrate every feature** — interpretations of nose length, eye depth, skin pigmentation, brow ridge, jaw width all shift against the phenotypic baseline. A "long nose" varies by phenotype.
- **Mixed phenotype is normal** — most people are contact types. Name the contributing parents.
- **Note where the subject diverges from baseline** — those divergences are the individual signals.

Confidence: this is the murkiest step. Photos rarely give you the skeletal data needed for high confidence. Hedge.

## Taxonomy — the three parent groups

### Nordid

**Defining cues**
- Depigmented skin; the rare phenotype with multiple eye colors (blue, green, hazel, gray) and multiple hair colors (blonde, red, lighter browns)
- Skull: **dolichocephalic AND large** ("big skulls and craniums") — the size distinguishes Nordid dolichocephaly from Mediterranid dolichocephaly
- Long features (leptomorphic), strong chins, deep-set eyes, heavy brow ridges on the Cro-Magnoid pole
- Long noses, especially on the Indo-European pole

**Body**: tall, long-limbed, often muscular.

**Cultural framing** (Prosopa's): the warrior/aristocratic type of European history. "The warrior caste throughout European history is highly Nordid… wherever the Nordic man is, he wants to fight." Indo-European / Aryan / Yamnaya lineage.

**Sub-types** (spectrum: Cro-Magnoid pole → Indo-European pole):

| Sub-type | Cues | Worked examples |
|---|---|---|
| **Dalofaelid** | Continental Cro-Magnoid; Germany, parts of Norway/Sweden; square face, robust, deep-set eyes | Brad Pitt |
| **Brünn** | Insular Cro-Magnoid; Britain, especially Ireland; big, robust, muscular, broad faces, heavy brow ridges, rounder than Dalofaelid | JFK, Colm Meaney, Jeremy Clarkson |
| **Borreby** | Independently alpinized Cro-Magnoid; broader/shorter skull, shorter nose, more infantile features; Northern European fishing populations; "peasant" not warrior origin | Werner Herzog, Schopenhauer, Gerald Ford, Marilyn Monroe |
| **Trønder** | Cro-Magnoid × Indo-European; Viking phenotype; Norway, Iceland, Sweden, Denmark, diaspora | Kevin Bacon |
| **Anglo-Saxon** | Brünn-side Cro-Magnoid × possible brachycephalic; Netherlands and England; softer, more gracile than Trønder | Michael Caine, Sean Bean, Alec Guinness |
| **Keltic Nordid / Celtic-Nord** | Bell-Beaker origin; long faces, very high foreheads, very long large prominent noses; "most classic British type" | Kevin Costner, Bob Odenkirk, Woodrow Wilson, H.P. Lovecraft, J.R.R. Tolkien |
| **Hallstatt Nordid** | Furthest Indo-European pole; "most typical Nord"; very long thin face, gracile, tall, thin, soft, "almost sensitive looking"; classic Swedish | Max von Sydow |
| **East Nordid / Nordid proper** | Slavic counterpart to Hallstatt | — |
| **Proto-Nordid** | Hallstatt-like + more robust; matches IE grave-site bone measurements — "what the ancient Aryans looked like" | — |
| **Indo-Nordid** | India, Afghanistan; highly concentrated in the Kalash | — |
| **Udmurt / Volga Nordid** | Non-IE Uralic-speaking; world's highest red-hair concentration; very robust | — |

**Nordid contact types** (also listed under partner phenotypes):
- **Sub-Alpinid** (Nordid × Alpinid) — central/western Germany, Alsace, France
- **Noric** (Nordid × Dinarid) — "blonde Dinarid"; France, Austria, N. Italy, Balkans, E. Europe
- **North Atlantid** (Nordid × Atlanto-Mediterranid) — "snow-white Henry Cavill complexion"
- **North Pontid** (Nordid × Pontid × Baltid) — Eastern Slavs (Russians, Ukrainians, Belarusians)

### Alpinid

**Defining cues**
- Pale skin
- Darker hair (brown to black; lighter when mixed with Nordid)
- Brown eyes (green/blue if mixed)
- Skull: **brachycephalic but tall vertically**; high foreheads
- Face: round, somewhat flat
- Nose: short, snub
- Overall: infantile / "almost baby-like" features

**Body**: short and stocky; limbs short relative to torso; endomorphic tendency — "the main people in Europe who get stocky and large."

**Cultural framing**: the stable, sedentary, settled-farmer / peasant archetype. "A population associated with farmers, people who live very stable, static and stationary sort of lives." **Alpanization** is the evolutionary process: low-energy farming life produces broader/shorter skulls, shorter noses, infantile features.

**Sub-types**:

| Sub-type | Cues | Worked examples |
|---|---|---|
| **West Alpinid** | The technical name for the central-European core; canonical Alpinid | Martin Luther |
| **Sub-Nordid** | Alpinid × Nordid contact; central/western Germany, Alsace, Austria; mostly Alpinid in shape with slightly longer faces and lighter coloring | Adolf Hitler ("very typical Austrian") |
| **Sub-Alpinid** | Alpinid × Mediterranid south of the Alps (here "sub" = south of) | Joe Rogan, James Gandolfini |
| **Kelto-Alpinid** | Celtic-Nordid × Alpinid; long-faced, long-nosed | Mozart |
| **Gorid** | Poland / western Ukraine; Alpinid–Baltid intermediate | — |
| **Strandid** | "Stranded type"; Scandinavian fjord/coastal Alpinid | Notch (Markus Persson), Greta Thunberg |
| **East Alpinid** | Anatolia, Armenia; convergent-evolution mountain Alpinid | — |
| **African Alpinid** | Morocco, Algeria; convergently evolved from Berberid base | — |

**Convergent evolution thesis**: Alpinid shape is "perhaps the most energy efficient shape to have if you are a settled farming population." Unrelated mountain-farmer populations across Europe, North Africa, Asia converge on it.

### Mediterranid

**Defining cues**
- Skin: fair or light brown
- Hair: brown or black
- Eyes: brown or black
- Skull: **dolichocephalic but small** — long-narrow-and-small (the key contrast with Nordid)
- Face: longer and narrower; **gracile, refined, soft**; less robust/craggy than Cro-Magnoid Nordids

**Body**: shorter on average; thinner; gracile build.

**Cultural framing**: the civilization-builder / urban / agricultural archetype. "The race that invented civilization. We was Rome. We was Greece." Prosopa treats Mediterranid as the **basal Caucasian / default Eurasian type** — the baseline from which Nordid and Alpinid diverged.

**Aesthetic note** (verbatim): "The Gracile Mediterranid type is one of the most beautiful. The best babes are Gracile Meds."

**Sub-types**:

| Sub-type | Cues | Worked examples |
|---|---|---|
| **Gracile Mediterranid** | Canonical Med; small nose, big eyes, soft refined features; Iberia, southern France, Italy, Greece, Turkey, even Britain | Rowan Atkinson, Prosopa himself |
| **Trans-Mediterranid** | N. African Gracile Med; longer/narrower face, bigger eyes, slightly darker; Libya, Morocco, Algeria, Tunisia, Sicily, southern Spain | — |
| **Berberid** | Ancient Cro-Magnoid Mediterranid; indigenous Paleolithic North African; Morocco, Algeria | — |
| **Canarid** | Lighter Berberid; Canary Islanders, Kabyle of Algeria; blond/blue-eyed element | — |
| **Atlanto-Mediterranid / Eurafricanid** | Robust Med; Atlantic Europe + N. Africa; strong in Wales, Portugal, Morocco; Balkans, Bulgaria, Turkey, Mesopotamia | — |
| **Proto-Iranid** | Eastern counterpart of Atlanto-Mediterranid; robust, primitive; Persians, Turks, Kurds | — |
| **Pontid** | "East Mediterranean"; eastern Atlanto-Med around Black Sea; Bulgaria, Turkey, Ukraine, Russia, Romania; matches IE kurgan skulls | — |
| **Paleo-Sardinian** | Proto-Med; Sardinia, Crete; closely matches original Anatolian farmers | — |
| **Basque** | Mostly Med + Dinarid/Alpinid mountain adaptation; only pre-IE-speaking European population; black hair, big nose, short broad skull | — |
| **Litorid** | Dinarized Med; long narrow face, shorter skull, very prominent hooked nose; coasts from N. Africa through Britain to Sweden/Poland | — |

**Mediterranid contact types**:
- **Sub-Alpinid** (Med × Alpinid south of the Alps) — Italy especially. Examples: Gandolfini, Joe Rogan
- **North Atlantid** (Atlanto-Mediterranid × Nordid) — Western European Atlantic. Example: Henry Cavill
- **Paleo-Atlantid** (Med × Nord with Cro-Magnon recall) — isolated Irish/Scottish/Norwegian fjords; over-represented among Irish-Americans. Example: young Tommy Lee Jones
- **North Pontid** (Pontid × Nordid × Baltid) — Belarus, Poland, Ukraine
- **Anatolid** (Med × Armenoid) — Turkey, Lebanon, Syria

**Sister Mediterranoid types** (not Med subsets — sister races on the broader Mediterranoid spectrum):
- **Orientalid** — Arab Middle Eastern
- **Indid** — Northern India

## Other -id labels Prosopa references

These appear across his work but aren't profiled in the four foundational videos:

- **Dinarid** — partner in Noric Nordid (Dinarized blonde Nord), Litorid (Dinarized Med), Basque
- **Baltid** — Eastern European, northern-influenced, slight East-Asian tinge; partner in North Pontid and Gorid
- **Armenoid** — Anatolian/Caucasus partner type in Anatolid (Med + Armenoid) and Litorid genesis. Cues from the Nick Reiner reading: protruding eyes, long curved nose, short and broad head
- **Cro-Magnid / Cro-Magnoid** — ancestral category for Western Hunter-Gatherers and the Cro-Magnoid pole of Nordid; also the implicit ancestor of Alpinid (via alpanization) and Berberid

## Historical revision Prosopa stresses

The older neat narrative — Mediterraneans = pre-Aryan Anatolian farmers; Nordids = invading Indo-Europeans — is partially wrong:
- Early European Farmer (EEF) DNA actually carries genes for **lighter** skin
- Yamnaya DNA carries genes for **darker** hair and skin
- Many Yamnaya kurgan skulls are robust Mediterranid, not Nordid

The synthesis: modern Mediterraneans = Paleo-Med (Sardinian-like) EEF stock + a robust-but-still-dark southern Yamnaya influence. Nordids emerge only when more-blond Yamnaya migrate north and mix with blue-eyed Western Hunter-Gatherers in dark Northern European climates.
