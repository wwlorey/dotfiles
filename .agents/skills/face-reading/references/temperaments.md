# The Four Temperaments (Prosopa's reading from the side profile)

Read from the side profile. Forehead carries the **intellect's** temperament; chin carries the **initial energy of action**; jaw carries the **long-term endurance energy of action**. These can disagree — Prosopa explicitly allows compound calls like "choleric with a sanguine forehead" or "sanguine jaw, sanguine chin, but with a choleric forehead." Name the compound when it fits.

The side-profile **curvature overlay** is independent: convex (bird/bullet) = "bow of melancholy"; flat = balanced; concave ("moonman crescent") = dullness.

---

## Choleric — "Builder of the World" / "Positive Pessimist"

**Side-profile cues** (verbatim Prosopa):
- High, rounded forehead (or "high, straight" depending on the read)
- Chin level with the forehead, often slightly jutting
- Obtuse angle to the jawline
- Acute or strong jaw angle in some reads

**Trait cluster**
- Willful, bossy, commanding, decisive
- Thinks, plans, and acts in the **long term**
- "Builders of the world"
- "Positive pessimists" — see all the problems but it galvanizes them to act
- Deep thinker, intellectual, austere, work-focused
- High libido, low need for sleep
- Can intellectualize and be emotionally detached

**As a forehead only** (compound reading)
- Adds long-term intellectual planning to whatever temperament drives the jaw and chin
- Choleric forehead + sanguine energy of action = wants to plan/build but is impulsive in execution
- Choleric forehead + sanguine forehead is the "real danger zone" pattern Prosopa flags — quick rash decision + powerful follow-through

**Shadow** (Prosopa's tone): cold, harsh, contemptuous, domineering, hard-driving.

---

## Sanguine — "Charismatic" / "Energizer Bunny"

**Side-profile cues**
- Tilted-back (sloping) forehead
- 90° jawline angle
- Strong protruding chin

**Trait cluster**
- Charming, impulsive, outgoing, excitable
- Quick intellect, impatient, snap decisions
- Once committed: can be meticulous and willing to put in long hours
- Athletic, body-coordinated; reddish skin tone in classical mappings
- "Killer instinct" when paired with choleric intellect

**As a chin only / initial energy of action**
- Initial energy: outgoing, enthusiastic, positive, off-the-cuff
- The person presents as warm and quick on first meeting

**Shadow**: scattered, impulsive, attention-seeking, conflict-averse.

---

## Phlegmatic — "Slow and Steady Turtle" / "Negative Optimist"

**Side-profile cues**
- Rounded or straight forehead
- Chin level with the forehead, no projection
- Indistinct jawline (soft transition, no sharp angle)
- Often heavier, fleshier overall build

**Trait cluster**
- Calm, enduring, supportive, emotionally stable
- Patient, tolerant, hard to rattle
- Long-term endurance of the choleric paired with chill, stable energy of action
- "More of the slow and steady turtle type, versus the very rapid, energizer bunny choleric"
- **"Negative optimists"** — the dyadic mirror to choleric "positive pessimists." Cholerics see all the problems and it galvanizes them; phlegmatics see all the problems and reassure themselves things will work out.
- Reliable, loyal; the **second-in-command** archetype; great VPs, secretaries of state, chiefs of staff, treasurers
- Prosopa's note: **most clergymen are phlegmatic** — Jupiter face + phlegmatic = canonical priest / pastor / imam / rabbi pattern
- Prosopa's note from FACE-OFF Ep 1: "If you want to pick a president, pick a choleric. A phlegmatic type is not going to cut it. VP, yeah. Secretary of state, maybe. Chief of staff, treasurer, awesome. President, heck no."

**With "bow of melancholy" overlay**: adds creativity, sensitivity, quickness of wit

**Shadow**: passive, conflict-avoidant, slow to act on warnings, complacent.

---

## Melancholic — "Creative Visionary" / "Sensitive Reactor"

**Side-profile cues**
- Receding chin (sits behind a vertical line dropped from the forehead)
- Tilted-back (sloping) forehead
- Downward jawline from ear to chin (descending)
- Often pairs with a **convex** side-profile curve (the "bow of melancholy")

**Trait cluster**
- Sensitive, imaginative, idealistic, moody
- Nervous, reactive, pessimistic, attention-seeking
- Rich inner life; energy turned inward
- Quick wit when paired with sanguine elements

**As a chin only / initial energy of action**
- "Melancholic initial energy of action" — presents as witty, charming, funny, quick-thinking, then turns cold, reserved, austere, bossy, overbearing over time

**Shadow**: rumination, withdrawal, perfectionism, dramatic self-narrative.

## Side-profile curvature overlay

Independent of the four-humor call:

- **Convex** (bird-shaped or bullet-shaped) — "bow of melancholy" → sensitivity, reactivity, creativity, imagination, quick wit
- **Flat** — neither sensitive nor dull
- **Concave** ("moonman crescent") — powerful intellect + powerful energy of action + withdrawn midface = dullness, unperceptiveness, overbearingness

This is Prosopa's quick read of how the temperament expresses through emotional texture.

---

## How Prosopa stacks compound temperaments

In multiple videos he reads each region separately:

- **Forehead** = intellect → carries one humor
- **Chin** = initial energy of action → carries another (or the same)
- **Jaw / jawline** = long-term endurance energy of action → carries another (or the same)

The full call is e.g. "sanguine jaw, sanguine chin, but with a choleric forehead" (Nick Reiner). Use this stacking when the regions visibly disagree.

Pattern Prosopa flags:
- **Choleric intellect + sanguine energy of action** → tension; wants to plan/build but can't execute patiently. He reads this as a frustration pattern.
- **Choleric jaw + sanguine forehead** → "the real danger zone" (his words on Tyler Robinson video).

---

## Sign-off vocabulary

Prosopa closes temperament calls with: "builders of the world," "positive pessimists" (choleric); "killer instinct" (sanguine forehead + choleric body); "energizer bunny" vs "slow and steady turtle" (choleric vs phlegmatic). Use this vocabulary in the reading — it's his.
