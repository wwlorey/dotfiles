# Feature Catalog (Prosopa transcripts only)

For Step 4 of the reading. Every entry comes from a Prosopa Insights YouTube video — primarily the Carl Jung, Pope Leo, Sydney Sweeney, Tyler Robinson, Nick Reiner reads and the Physiognomy 101 explainers (Philtrum, Nose, Eyes).

Use the function-driven rule Prosopa repeats: **an organ's meaning is related to its function**. Eyes see → perception. Nose presents first → general behavior and disposition. Mouth speaks → communication. Philtrum bridges midface and lower face → expression of inner life into the physical world.

---

## Facial thirds (Prosopa's foundational frame)

- **Upper third** (eyebrow to hairline) = intellectual faculties
- **Middle third / midface** (eyebrow to bottom of nose) = social and emotional faculties
- **Lower third** (bottom of nose to chin) = material concerns, hedonism, pleasure, money

Dominant zone = where the person's interests lie. Cite this in the gestalt.

---

## Inner face vs outer face (compartmentalization axis)

A gestalt-level observation Prosopa treats as one of his most consequential calls when present. Read the **ratio** of inner-face area (brow line to bottom lip, between the inner edges of the cheekbones) to outer-face area (hairline to chin × ear to ear).

| Cue | Reading |
|---|---|
| **Small inner face relative to outer** | Compartmentalization, aloofness, stress immunity; "two minds at once" — can do one thing while mentally elsewhere; capacity for doublespeak. Often advantageous for politicians, pundits, operators. |
| **Large inner face relative to outer** | Social attunement, sensitivity to others' emotional states, less compartmentalization. |
| **Balanced** | Inner and outer aligned — no compartmentalization read. |

Worked examples Prosopa cites: small inner face — Charlie Kirk, Jeffrey Epstein, Bill Clinton. Large inner face — Ghislaine Maxwell.

When the inner face is dramatically small or large, **lead the synthesis with it** — Prosopa treats it as the spine of the read when present.

## Eyebrows

The eyebrows are Prosopa's "decision-making energy reserve." Two axes: **length = planning vs action**; **shape = which factors weigh in the decision**.

| Cue | Reading |
|---|---|
| Thick / bushy | Intense energy in decision-making, stamina, "maybe kind of overbearing" |
| Thin | Gentle, refined, contemplative decision-making |
| **Length axis** | |
| Long (extending past eye) | Premeditated, likes to plan ahead |
| Short | Spontaneous, impulse-driven, action-before-planning |
| Medium / average | Balance of planning and quick action |
| **Set height** | |
| Low-set (close to eye) | Quick to judge, snap decisions, "very masculine decision-making style," commits and sticks |
| High-set (above orbit) | Discerning, slow to decide, more reflection between sensing and acting; "highbrow" |
| **Shape axis** | |
| Straight (flat across brow) | Stubborn, logical, fact-based decision-maker; rational, independent (not consensus-driven) |
| Curved / rounded | People-oriented; consensus-based decisions; "extroverted feeling (FE)" in MBTI; harmonious, peaceful; learns by example; culturally coded as the "feminine" eyebrow |
| Arched (triangular) | Decisions oriented to control, competitiveness, power; jealous; "mercurial" — ties to Mercury face |
| "Spock" (sharp upward angle) | Impulsivity, irresponsibility |
| Droopy (drooping outward/downward) | Doubt, paranoia (Prosopa also links this to schizophrenic patterns in the Nick Reiner read) |
| Asymmetric — more curved on the right | Public persona has become more empathetic / consensus-based; inner self (left) carries the harder mode |
| **Cosmetic modifications** | |
| Thinned (waxed / plucked) | Reads as less intense, more checked out, calm |
| Cosmetically rounded | Reads as more considerate, empathetic, feminine |

## Eyes

Function: **perception, worldview** primary; emotion, social connection, relationships secondary. "The windows of the soul" — Prosopa's most-used framing.

| Cue | Reading |
|---|---|
| Big eyes | Innocent, naïve, "wide-eyed," takes things in uncritically |
| Small "beady" eyes | Cynical, untrusting, perceptive, critical, "analytical eyes" |
| Almond-shaped (flat lower lid) | Sees world through intellect |
| Round (curved lower lid) | Sees world through morals and emotions |
| Squinty (any shape) | Extra cautious worldview, looking for dangers |
| Deep-set | Withdrawn, calculating, "watching from a cave"; thinks before speaking; testosterone-linked masculine cue ("hunter eyes") |
| Protruding / bulging | Socially driven; speaks off the cuff; wants to be heard; may "blurt things out inappropriately" |
| Close-set | Detail-focused, nitpicky, judgmental, less tolerant of error |
| Wide-set | Tolerant, big-picture, "spacey," bad with details |
| **Positive canthal tilt** (outer corner up) | Confident, driven, assertive, "puts own interests above others"; rebellious |
| **Negative canthal tilt** (outer corner down) | People-pleaser, conciliatory, puts others' interests over own |
| Asymmetric canthal tilt | Inner-vs-outer split — positive on left = privately driven; negative on right = publicly conciliatory |
| Full upper eyelid | Strong need for connection and intimacy; wanted to belong |
| Asymmetric eyelid fullness | Left fuller than right = wants to overshare about self, doesn't care to learn about others |
| **Yin sanpaku** (white visible below iris) | Under heavy stress, ton of pressure, ready to explode |
| **Yang sanpaku** (white visible above iris) | Wants power over others; Prosopa: "naturally a psychopathic killer" |
| Thick / heavy upper eyelid | Strong need for connection and intimacy |
| Droopy left eyelid only | Shame or avoidance about something in personal / private life; newer onset suggests current secret rather than childhood trauma |

## Nose

Function: "the pillar of the face… the most forward-reaching part… presents first when you meet people or do things out in the world." Reads **general behavior and disposition**.

| Cue | Reading |
|---|---|
| Long nose | Patient, thoughtful, contemplative, oriented toward thinking; "long of nose" in the literal Hebrew Exodus sense |
| Short nose | Action-oriented, impatient, "doesn't want to just sit around and think" |
| **Greek / straight** | Cool-headed, rational, not driven by deep passions, "stone cold" |
| **Roman / Aquiline / hooked eagle** | Conquering spirit, "born to command" |
| **Egyptian / evenly curved (parrot beak)** | Deep, rich passions; mystical sensitivity; common in priests, artists, poets — NOT a "true Roman" (conquering) read |
| **Beaked / convex nose** | Deep passions and sensitivity (distinct from Roman aquiline) |
| Bump on the bridge | Reactivity, impulsivity, snapping under stress |
| No bump, flat bridge | Not reactive, not impulsive, doesn't snap |
| **Wide bridge** | Intense main physical energy, high stamina, endurance ("energy reserve") |
| **Narrow bridge** | Sensitivity, "finickiness," less stamina translating ideas to social / emotional life |
| Long vertical nose | Thoughtful, more about thinking than acting |
| Projecting / forward nose | "Adept at getting what he wanted out of life" |
| Withdrawn / flat nose | Hesitant to impose self on life |
| Round nose tip | Generosity and kindness |
| Pointy nose tip | Naturally mean / miserly / harsh in personal life (Prosopa frames as desirable in a leader) |
| **Round nostrils** | Sensual and romantic disposition |
| **Long nostrils** | Intellectual disposition |

## Mouth and Lips

Function: communication.

- **Upper lip** = relationship with the outside world
- **Lower lip** = relationship with own inner world / sense of self

| Cue | Reading |
|---|---|
| Full upper lip | Expressive, open, "an open book" |
| Thin upper lip | Hesitant to communicate what he truly thinks / feels; poor relationship with the outside world; "calculated with his public statements"; "stiff upper lip" |
| Full lower lip | Strong sense of self; in touch with inner world; seen in "passionate artists and poets, musicians" |
| Thin lower lip | Weak sense of self; out of touch with own thoughts / feelings / morals / desires; needs to talk to others to understand self; in extreme cases "no real me," "actual NPC in a suit" |
| Wide mouth | Disciplined, self-controlled, work-focused, ambitious, "wants what he wants"; talkative, outgoing |
| **Wide "frog mouth"** | Strong sensual / sexual appetite (Epstein read) |
| Small / narrow mouth | Quiet, reserved, calculated, introverted; deep-set eyes + small mouth = thinks before speaking, doesn't talk often |
| Mouth corners upturned | Optimistic, sociable |
| Mouth corners downturned | Pessimistic by nature, "maybe even a little bitter" |
| **Lipless mouth corners** (lips stop before the mouth does) | **Executive leadership capacity.** Prosopa: "Out of all the facial features of the 200 richest people in the world, this is the only one that they all share." Seen in billionaires, CEOs, great leaders. |
| **Sun upper lip** (wide, low-arch, no prominent cupid's bow) | Cold, blunt, distant, detached communication style; "just the facts" |
| **"Venus lip" / strong cupid's bow** | Smooth-talking, silver-tongued |
| Defined / peaked cupid's bow | Articulate, expressive, warmth in delivery |
| Weak / undefined cupid's bow | Cold, detached communication; must rely on scripts |
| Lower lip fuller than upper (≈80% of people) | Other-directed; needs others' input to understand self |
| Upper lip fuller than lower (≈20%) | Self-directed sense of self |
| Gummy smile (exposed gums) | "Insecure about ability to be loved," seeks romantic relationships for validation; in men, becoming "a professional political pundit" (Charlie Kirk read) |
| Big teeth | "Gets what he wants out of life"; doesn't shy from fulfilling desires |
| Big teeth + big gums | Desperate for affection and approval; wants tight-knit relationships |
| Small teeth + big gums | Same desperate-for-love pattern, gentler intensity |
| Gap between front teeth | Unconventional, independent, "did not care for the expectations society put on him" |

## Philtrum

Function: bridge between midface (social-emotional) and lower face (material). The face-as-funnel: intellect → decision-making → behavior → expression *here*.

### Depth (energy reserve axis)

| Cue | Reading |
|---|---|
| Deep philtrum | Strong link between social-emotional and physical/practical world; "strongly and readily expresses passions, emotions and social feelings"; intense energy and stamina |
| Shallow philtrum | "Narrow bottleneck for energy, emotion, thoughts"; underlying intensity has trouble expressing itself in the physical world; "kind of detached, kind of out of it" |
| Shallow or non-existent | Physical symptom of fetal alcohol syndrome and developmental disorders (Prosopa) |

### Width (principles vs pleasure axis) — Prosopa rates this "100% true"

| Cue | Reading |
|---|---|
| Wide philtrum | High sexual impulse; pleasure over principle; hedonistic |
| Narrow philtrum | Strong moral impulse; principles over pleasure; "very moralistic, kind of prudish even" |

### Length (motor activity axis) — Prosopa qualifies as uncertain

| Cue | Reading |
|---|---|
| Long philtrum | High aptitude for motor activity, hand-eye coordination, sports (cited from "Dr. Peter Resnik, *Face Reading Secrets for Successful Relationships*") |
| Short philtrum | Less inclined to motor activity. Prosopa: "I've seen mixed results with this from clients" — flag as moderate confidence. |

### Coverage

| Cue | Reading |
|---|---|
| Mustache (covering philtrum) | "Closing off of the social and emotional world from the material world"; either self-control or coldness/detachment (Prosopa cites Amish and Salafi as cultural parallels) |

## Asymmetry shortcuts

Asymmetry has its own step (Step 5) but recurs in feature notes:

- **Left side** = inner self / who they came into the world as / private
- **Right side** = outer self / who they became / public mask
- **Convergence** = inner-and-outer aligned (alignment is itself a finding)
- **Divergence** = "predator disguised as prey" / "wolf in sheep's clothing" — when the halves look like two different characters
- **Brain-hemisphere mechanism**: right brain (authentic, spontaneous) → left side of face; left brain (calculated, persona) → right side. Repeated asymmetric expressions embed into the face over time.
- **Split face shape**: one half a pure archetype, other half a pure different archetype, with no mixing (Mark Carney = pure Saturn right / pure Mars left).
- **Asymmetric canthal tilt**: positive on left + negative on right = privately driven & assertive, publicly conciliatory (Pope Leo).
- **Asymmetric eyelid fullness**: left fuller than right = wants to overshare about self but doesn't care to learn about others (Sydney Sweeney).
- **Asymmetric eyebrow shape**: right brow more curved over time = public persona has shifted toward consensus / empathy while inner self carries the harder mode.
- Inherent asymmetry vs expressive asymmetry: two layers — feature asymmetry (bone) and expression asymmetry (muscle). Multiple photos needed to distinguish.
