# The Planetary Personality Archetypes (Prosopa's reading from face shape, front view)

Read from face shape front-on. Each archetype is the **broad strokes of personality** — Prosopa frames it as "the archetype that they fulfill and inhabit." Compound forms are legitimate and often more accurate.

Temperament (Step 2) is constitutional; archetype (this step) is how the temperament tends to show up in a recognizable character. Prosopa reads **misfit** between archetype and temperament as someone who "lost the plot" or whose life forced a non-native channeling.

---

## Sun — Oval

**Face cue**: oval face, balanced curves, no zone strongly dominant.

**Archetype**: "aloof aristocrat."

**Trait cluster**
- Gregarious, friendly, charismatic, but **aloof, detached, above it all**
- Idealistic, analytical, big-picture, sees the whole
- Stress-resistant
- Performs better with recognition; sulks or boasts without it

**Alchemical note** (Prosopa uses this in the Jung video): gold metal. The "transmutation" pattern is Saturn → Sun (lead → gold) — a person who matures from Saturnian discipline into Solar elevation. Jung's career was Prosopa's example.

---

## Moon — Round / Full

**Face cue**: round face, full cheeks, wide-set eyes, soft contours.

**Trait cluster**
- Family-oriented, imaginative
- Strong inner life; intuition over analysis
- Caretaker by default; deeply loyal to a small circle
- Emotionally rich; sometimes unstable

(Prosopa references Moon less frequently than the other archetypes in indexed videos — use sparingly.)

---

## Mars — Rectangular / Triangular (narrow chin)

**Face cue**: rectangular face or triangular narrowing toward the chin.

**Archetype**: "warrior archetype."

**Trait cluster**
- Naturally **sanguine** (Prosopa's pairing): active, gregarious
- Urge to fight; "and sometimes to kill" (his words on Nick Reiner)
- Decisive in conflict, willing to push
- Independent operator

Mars often sublimates into competitive professions: athletes, fighters, surgeons, soldiers.

---

## Mercury — Long Rectangular / Long Triangle

**Face cue**: long rectangular or long triangle; long nose, often long chin; mobile expressive features.

**Archetype**: "jester" / "creative attention-seeker."

**Trait cluster**
- Sensitive, creative, attention-seeking, reactive, nervous
- Sharp communicator; mercurial mood and tone shifts
- "Cry for help" energy when over-emphasized

**Prosopa's caveat** (Tyler Robinson video): "Beware a small-faced Mercury, unless it's Ryan Gosling, of course."

---

## Jupiter — Broad / Reverse Trapezoid

**Face cue**: broad face, often widest at the forehead/cheekbones, narrowing toward the chin (reverse trapezoid).

**Archetype**: "clergyman archetype" — the most common face shape for clergy across denominations (priests, pastors, preachers, imams, rabbis, per Prosopa's Pope Leo video).

**Trait cluster**
- Moralistic, self-righteous, **overbearing in their will**
- Outgoing, outspoken, gregarious, friendly
- **Jolly, jovial, "great at parties"**
- Naturally paired with **phlegmatic** temperament for the canonical clergy pattern

**Clergy temperament outlier — the choleric-clergyman pattern.** Most clergy are phlegmatic+Jupiter (priest-VP, mediator, "great at parties"). The choleric+Jupiter outlier — Pope Leo XIV, the Renaissance cardinal-CEO archetype — inverts every downstream synthesis: it's the bishop-CEO, the institutional builder, the executive clergyman. When reading a clergy face do not default toward phlegmatic; check whether the side-profile cues (high rounded forehead, chin level with forehead, obtuse-to-acute jaw angle, no bow of melancholy) point to the choleric call instead.

**Shadow**: pompous, sermonizing.

---

## Venus — Diamond / Heart / Lozenge

**Face cue**: diamond, heart, or lozenge shape; wider at the cheekbones; refined features.

**Archetype**: "romantic diplomat."

**Trait cluster**
- Interested in three domains: **(1) romance and relationships, (2) beauty and aesthetics, (3) social causes**
- "Great interior decorators"
- "Great activists and advocates"
- Naturally **sanguine** temperament pairing
- Choleric + Venus → tendency toward promiscuity (Prosopa's Sydney Sweeney reading)

**Shadow**: unreliable romantically, vain.

---

## Saturn — Trapezoidal / Tall Lean

**Face cue**: trapezoidal face (broad square forehead narrowing to square chin), or tall lean rectangular with deep-set eyes.

**Archetype**: "diligent accountant archetype."

**Trait cluster**
- Hyper-choleric, very focused on work
- Detail-oriented, meticulous, diligent
- Time-conscious
- Nitpicky, can be cold

**Alchemical note**: lead metal. Saturn → Sun is the transmutation pattern.

---

## Earth / Terran — Square

**Face cue**: square face, full cheeks, strong jaw.

**Archetype**: "farmer archetype."

**Trait cluster**
- Steady, stable, direct, logical
- Focused on facts and tangible things
- Grounded, practical
- Hard-working

Pairs cleanly with phlegmatic or choleric temperaments.

---

## Uranus — Long Trapezoid

**Face cue**: long trapezoidal face.

**Archetype**: "radical thinker."

**Trait cluster**
- Stubborn, diligent, time-conscious
- Ideas ahead of their time
- Strong sexual impulse
- "Doesn't care about others" — willing to be unliked for the work

Often discussed alongside Mercury for long-faced subjects (Prosopa's Tyler Robinson video offers Mercury vs Uranus as competing reads for a long thin face).

## Compound archetypes (Prosopa's signature pattern)

He routinely names two archetypes when a single shape is too clean. Examples from the corpus:

- **Saturn over Mars** — rectangular face narrowing trapezoidally to chin → "Martian aggressive warlike energy channeled through this narrow focused Saturnian channel" → can produce surgeons or military men when channeled well; the **murderous-impulse pattern** when not. Prosopa cites OJ Simpson, Michael C. Hall / Dexter.
- **Earth + Jupiter** = **"Regulator" archetype** — square jaw plus broad upper face → grounded practical activism (Earth) + moralistic gregarious clergyman energy (Jupiter). JD Vance read in FACE-OFF.
- **Venus + Sun** — primary Venus shape with oval (Sun) overlay → romantic diplomat with aloof-aristocrat air. Sydney Sweeney read.

When naming a compound, the dominant archetype goes first. Tie the mix back to the temperament call from Step 2.

## Shadow archetypes

Prosopa allows **"shadow [archetype]"** as a named pattern: same archetype, **inverted moral valence**. Ghislaine Maxwell is his canonical example — a "shadow Venus" who has all Venus traits (diplomatic, smooth-talking, born-seductive) "but it's in service of evil." Use the shadow modifier when a clear archetypal face is paired with antisocial behavior or purpose.

## The aging trajectory (Venus → Mars)

Prosopa reads the face as drifting over decades. The recurring pattern: **Venus → Mars as the face sags downward with age.** Donald Trump in his youth was a Venus (big dealmaker); now he reads as Mars (warlike). This isn't a betrayal of constitution — it's how soft tissue settling reveals or shifts the dominant archetype. Note recency of the photo when calling an archetype.

## The "persistent optical illusion" (Mercury → Saturn)

Prosopa's calibration caveat from FACE-OFF: a **pure choleric with a Mercury face shape** can persistently *read as Saturn*. The narrow focused Saturnian channel and the long Mercurial face look similar, but the constitutional driver is different. Cross-check with the temperament call — if the side profile reads pure choleric, lean Mercury over Saturn for the long-faced subject.

## Archetype ↔ Temperament fit

Prosopa reads the fit explicitly:

- **Saturn ↔ choleric** — natural pairing
- **Venus ↔ sanguine** — natural pairing
- **Mars ↔ sanguine** — natural pairing
- **Jupiter ↔ phlegmatic** — natural pairing for clergy
- **Mercury** — paired with melancholic, often a "cry for help" misfit
- **Sun ↔ phlegmatic** — natural pairing (stress-resistant calm), **with choleric undertones** carried forward from the Saturn → Sun transmutation lineage

A clean fit gets read as someone whose constitution and presentation align. A **misfit** ("Mercury face shape on a choleric-with-sanguine-forehead temperament" — Tyler Robinson) gets read as someone who "lost the plot," whose external archetype doesn't carry their internal energy well. A recurring misfit pattern: **Choleric + Venus** — the diplomatic-romantic shape driven by choleric fire instead of its native sanguine — reads as **promiscuity** (Prosopa's Sydney Sweeney reading). Name the fit in the synthesis.

## Job-fit / role-fit doctrine

Prosopa's FACE-OFF framework maps the temperament + archetype to a fit role. The pattern:

- **Choleric + Sun / Mars / Saturn** → leader / president / founder / general / CEO
- **Phlegmatic + Jupiter** → clergy, mediator, manager, VP, treasurer
- **Phlegmatic + Earth** → middle manager, second-in-command
- **Sanguine + Venus / Mercury** → salesman, communicator, entertainer
- **Sanguine + Mars** → warrior, athlete, fighter
- **Melancholic + Mercury** → artist, writer, designer
- **Melancholic + Saturn** → scholar, researcher, monk
- **Earth + Jupiter ("Regulator")** → senate, treasury, regulatory body — not presidency

Tie this to the synthesis — name the role the reading points to, not as prediction but as fit-pattern.

---

## Asymmetry-driven mixed archetypes

Prosopa sometimes reads different archetypes on each half of the face. Pope Leo: **Venian element on his right side** (sanguine, social, public) + **Saturnian element on his left side** (choleric, focused, private). When the halves diverge that strongly, name both halves' archetypes in Step 5 (Asymmetry) rather than forcing a single front-view call.
